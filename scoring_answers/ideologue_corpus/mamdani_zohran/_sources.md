## Collected

- `2025-06-24_democratic-primary-victory-speech.md` — Democratic primary victory speech, the night he defeated Cuomo. FDR/Mandela invocations; first cross-borough coalition statement (rev.com transcript).
- `2025-10-27_final-campaign-rally.md` — Largest closing-stretch rally (Washington Heights). Richest ideological synthesis of the campaign: Sankara/FDR/LaGuardia/MLK/Sanders references, rent freeze, free buses, universal childcare, anti-billionaire framing, ICE/deportation resistance (rev.com transcript).
- `2025-11-04_general-election-victory-speech.md` — General election victory speech as Mayor-elect. Direct address to Trump; explicit self-identification as young, Muslim, democratic socialist; Eugene Debs and Nehru invocations (rev.com transcript).
- `2026-01-01_mayoral-inaugural-address.md` — Inaugural address as 111th/112th Mayor. Most authoritative single statement of his governing program; lineage drawn to LaGuardia/Dinkins/de Blasio; explicit "I was elected as a democratic socialist and I will govern as a democratic socialist" framing (Good Morning America transcript; canonical nyc.gov URL blocked by WebFetch).
