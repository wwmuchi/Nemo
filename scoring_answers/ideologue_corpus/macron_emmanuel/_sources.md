# Emmanuel Macron — sources

**Tier:** T2 (French-native; Élysée publishes many official English translations)
**Language:** fr → en (prefer Élysée official translations; DeepL acceptable for less-load-bearing material)
**Era to anchor:** Presidency 2017–present.

## Key sources (a small focused set, not a survey)

Aim for ~4–6 documents that capture his Europe-centric worldview.
Suggested anchors:

- 2017-09-26 Sorbonne speech "Initiative for Europe" — load-bearing
  philosophical treatise on his Europe vision
- 2024-04-25 Sorbonne II "Europe — It Can Die" — the update / sequel,
  for current views
- 2024-05 The Economist interview — long-form on Europe / Russia / China
- 2019-11 The Economist interview ("NATO brain death") — the most
  quoted single foreign-policy framing

Stop once the picture is clear. Inaugurals, Davos addresses, and pension-
reform TV remarks largely restate themes covered above.

  prefer the French original + Élysée English translation pair.
