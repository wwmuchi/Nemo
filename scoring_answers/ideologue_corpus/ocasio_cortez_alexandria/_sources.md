**Language:** en
**Era to anchor:** Congressional tenure Jan 2019–present. 2018 primary

## Key sources (a small focused set, not a survey)

Aim for ~5–7 documents that capture her programmatic worldview.
Suggested anchors:

- 2019-02-07 Green New Deal resolution introduction (House floor + presser)
  — her flagship policy framing
- H. Res. 109 full text — the written program
- 2018-06 Intercept interview (right after primary win) — foundational
  ideological exposition
- One long-form podcast (Lex Fridman or Pod Save America) — extended
  unscripted format
- 2019 SXSW keynote — speech-format ideological synthesis
- One Instagram Live transcript on a load-bearing moment (e.g. post-Jan 6
  or post-2024 election) — uniquely candid format worth the transcription
  cost

Stop once the picture is clear. Floor speeches and committee questioning
clips are abundant but repetitive on themes already covered above.
