# Kim Jong Un — sources

**Tier:** T3 (sparse, state-mediated, heavily curated)
**Language:** ko → en (only state-approved English translations exist)
**Era to anchor:** Rule 2011–present, with emphasis on 2021+ (post-COVID
re-arming and Russia/China realignment).

## Key sources (a small focused set, not a survey)

Aim for ~4–6 documents that capture the regime's stated worldview.
Suggested anchors:

- 8th Party Congress (January 2021) opening report — defines post-COVID
  strategy, the single most load-bearing document
- One New Year address from the 2013–2020 run — annual statement of
  priorities in his earlier rhetorical mode
- 2023-09 Russia visit speeches (with Putin) — recent foreign-policy
  posture
- Singapore Summit joint statement (June 2018) — diplomatic-engagement phase

Stop there unless something clearly distinct is missing. State-mediated
output is repetitive on purpose; more documents add little.

## Notes — important caveats

- Everything is state-mediated and ghostwritten by the Workers' Party
  apparatus. Flag this in every `notes` field.
- KCNA's English translations sometimes soften or sharpen rhetoric vs the
  Korean original; cross-check with 38 North for load-bearing passages.
- For ideology scoring, retrieve with wider context windows since
  individual quotes carry more curation noise.

## Collected

- 2021-01-09_8th-party-congress-opening-report.md — KCNA official English summary of Kim's multi-day report to the 8th WPK Congress; the most load-bearing post-COVID strategy doc (Juche-economic self-reliance, ICBM/recon-satellite/tactical-nuclear targets, inter-Korean conditionality, "answer force with toughness" on the US). KCNA never released the full verbatim multi-day speech in English — this is the canonical English summary.
- 2019-01-01_new-year-address.md — KCNA official English translation of the 2019 New Year Address; last full New Year Address before he switched to Plenary reports in 2020. Annual statement of priorities in his earlier rhetorical mode.
- 2023-09-13_vostochny-russia-visit-toast-and-remarks.md — COMPILATION of verbatim Kim quotes from his Sept 12-13, 2023 Khasan arrival, Vostochny talks with Putin, and state dinner toast. No single full English transcript was released; this aggregates KCNA wires + Russian-side press readouts. Captures the post-COVID Russia-realignment posture.
- 2018-06-12_singapore-summit-joint-statement.md — Trump-Kim joint statement, signed primary text (English); diplomatic-engagement phase, "denuclearization" + peace-regime language. Co-authored with the US side, not unilateral Kim authorship.
