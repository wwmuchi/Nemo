## Border arrival at Khasan, Russian Far East (September 12, 2023)

Per KCNA, Kim Jong Un, on arriving at Khasan railway station and being greeted by Russian Natural Resources Minister Alexander Kozlov and Primorsky Territory Governor Oleg Kozhemyako, characterized the trip as a:

> "clear manifestation of the stand of the WPK and the government of the DPRK prioritizing the strategic importance of the DPRK-Russia relations."

He stated he was:

> "pleased to visit Russia again four years after his visit in 2019"

— describing it as "the first foreign visit after the worldwide public health crisis."

He extended:

> "deep thanks to the senior officials from the capital and local areas for greeting him with enthusiastic and warm feelings of friendship at the border station"

and conveyed:

> "best wishes to the president, government, army and people of the Russian Federation on behalf of the DPRK."

## Vostochny Cosmodrome talks with President Putin (September 13, 2023)

In the opening exchange with Putin at the cosmodrome, Kim said (per the Russian-side English transcript):

> "I am very glad that four years and five months later I had the opportunity to visit the Russian Federation, hold a meeting with President Putin."

> "Let me express to you, Comrade Putin, my sincere gratitude for the fact that you kindly invited us."

He emphasized that relations with the Russian Federation are "the very first priority" for the DPRK.

## State dinner toast (September 13, 2023, Vostochny)

Raising a toast at the official banquet, Kim declared:

> "We are confident that the Russian army and people will win a great victory in the just fight to punish evil groups who pursue hegemony, expansion, and ambition."

In a parallel KCNA-published rendering of the same toast:

> "I am certain that the Russian people and its military will emerge victorious in the fight to punish the evil forces that ambitiously pursues hegemony and expansion."

He further stated, without naming Ukraine:

> "The Russian military and its people will inherit the shining tradition of victory, confidently demonstrate invaluable dignity and honor on the fronts of the special military operation."

And pledged:

> "I will always be standing with Russia."

He characterized the bilateral tie as "an unbreakable strategic partnership" and said the visit "will be an important step in transforming the DPRK-Russia relationship."

He concluded the toast:

> "I propose to raise a glass to the good health of the respected President of the Russian Federation, comrade Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin! For new victories of great Russia, for the continuous development and strengthening of Korean-Russian friendship, for the health of all comrades present here!"

## Summary judgment of the visit (per KCNA wire summary)

Kim summarized the meeting as a vow to strengthen "strategic and tactical cooperation" between the two countries, and pledged "full and unconditional support" for Russia's war in Ukraine.
