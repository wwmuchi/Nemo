## Key sources (a small focused set, not a survey)

Aim for ~4–6 documents that capture his Austrian-school worldview.
Suggested anchors:

- 2024-01-17 World Economic Forum Davos speech — the single most
  important ideological document, a 20-minute distillation of his
  worldview (official English translation available)
- 2023-12-10 Inaugural address ("No hay plata") — opening statement of
  his program
- One long-form English-language interview (Tucker Carlson 2023 or Lex
  Fridman 2024) — extended exposition without translation cost
- One pre-political TV appearance on inflation / central banks —
  unconstrained-by-office version of his economics

Stop once the picture is clear. Additional UN / CPAC / Hayek Lecture
material largely restates the Davos themes.
