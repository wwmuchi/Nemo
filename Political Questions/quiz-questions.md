# Political & Ideological Quiz — Question Bank

>
> Each item is phrased as a statement on which a respondent would express agreement on a 5-point scale (Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree). Items are written in our own wording — for exact verbatim phrasing from a specific quiz, open that quiz directly (links in `quiz-links.md` and `political-quizzes.md`).

## Notes on Methodology

- **Deduplication**: Each opinion topic appears in only one category. Where the same policy area is multidimensional (e.g., abortion sits at the intersection of "civil liberties" and "family/sexuality"), it is placed under the category where it most naturally maps in political-science literature.
- **Phrasing convention**: All items are framed as affirmative statements to be answered on a Likert agreement scale (Strongly Disagree → Disagree → Neutral → Agree → Strongly Agree). To balance the questionnaire against acquiescence bias, ~50% of items can be reverse-scored.
- **Coverage**: 160 items across 16 categories, large enough to derive multiple political axes (economic, social, authority, globalism, religiosity, environmental, etc.).
